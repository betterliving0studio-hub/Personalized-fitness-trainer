Overview

Push-ups are one of the most effective and accessible bodyweight exercises. They target your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core muscles simultaneously. Perfect for both beginners and advanced athletes, push-ups improve upper-body strength, posture, and stability.

Benefits of Push-Ups

Strengthens chest, shoulders & triceps

Improves cardiovascular health

Enhances core stability & balance

Boosts functional fitness

How to Do Push-Ups

Step 1: Starting Position

Begin in a high plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart and your body forming a straight line from head to heels.

Step 2: Lower Down

Slowly bend your elbows to lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Keep your core tight and back flat.

Step 3: Push Up

Exhale as you push back up to the starting position, fully extending your arms. Avoid locking your elbows at the top.
